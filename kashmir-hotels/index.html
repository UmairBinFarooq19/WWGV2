Kashmir Accommodation

Best Hotels in Kashmir 2025

From the iconic floating houseboats on Dal Lake to luxury ski resorts in Gulmarg and peaceful riverside lodges in Pahalgam — Kashmir's accommodation is as varied as its landscapes. WildWays Global partners with handpicked hotels at every budget.

🏨 Hotels by Destination

🛶 Srinagar — Dal Lake Houseboats

CategoryPrice / Night
Standard Houseboat₹1,500–₹3,000
Deluxe Houseboat₹3,500–₹7,000
Luxury Houseboat₹8,000–₹18,000

Dal Lake houseboats are a uniquely Kashmiri experience — traditional cedar wood architecture, carved interiors, and shikara service. WildWays books only verified, certified houseboats.

🎿 Gulmarg — Ski Resort Hotels

CategoryPrice / Night
Standard Hotel (3★)₹2,500–₹5,000
Deluxe Hotel (4★)₹5,500–₹10,000
Luxury Resort (5★)₹12,000–₹28,000

Gulmarg hotels offer ski-in/ski-out convenience and panoramic mountain views. Book early for December–February — peak ski season fills up 2–3 months in advance.

🌿 Pahalgam — Forest Lodges

CategoryPrice / Night
Budget Guesthouse₹800–₹2,000
Riverside Hotel (3★)₹2,500–₹5,500
Resort (4★)₹6,000–₹14,000

Pahalgam lodges sit beside the Lidder River surrounded by pine forests. Most resorts offer mountain and river views. May–June requires advance booking.

⛺ Sonamarg — Camps & Hotels

CategoryPrice / Night
Basic Tent Camp₹1,000–₹2,500
Deluxe Camp₹3,000–₹6,000
Hotel (3★)₹3,500–₹8,000

Sonamarg accommodation ranges from basic camps beside the Sind River to proper hotels with heating — essential as nights are cold even in summer.
