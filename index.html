December–March: Snow, skiing in Gulmarg — magical winter wonderland. April–June: Blooming tulips, spring flowers, ideal weather — most popular season. July–September: Kashmir Great Lakes Trek, lush green meadows, monsoon rains. October–November: Golden chinar trees, clear skies, fantastic photography.

Kashmir tour packages start from ₹8,999 per person for 4N/5D covering Srinagar, Gulmarg, and Pahalgam. A 6N/7D complete package costs ₹14,999–₹19,999. Luxury packages with 5-star stays cost ₹32,000–₹50,000. Prices include hotel, meals, AC cab, and sightseeing.

The Kashmir Great Lakes Trek is India's most beautiful high-altitude trek — covering 7 pristine alpine lakes (Krishnasar, Vishansar, Gangabal, Nundkol, Gadsar, Satsar, Tarsar) over 7–8 days. The route goes from Sonamarg to Naranag, reaching 4,267m altitude. Best time: July–September. Cost: ₹14,999–₹18,999 per person with WildWays Global.

Gulmarg gondola (ropeway) prices in 2025: Phase 1 (Gulmarg to Kongdori): ₹900 per person. Phase 2 (Kongdori to Apharwat Peak): ₹1,050 per person. Both phases combined: ₹1,850. WildWays Global assists with advance gondola booking to avoid long queues.
