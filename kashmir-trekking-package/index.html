Trekking FAQs

The Gangabal Lake Trek (2–3 days) and Gulmarg Khilanmarg Trek (1–2 days) are ideal for beginners. Both offer stunning scenery without demanding long daily distances or extremely high altitudes.

A certified local guide is strongly recommended and often legally required for remote Kashmir treks. Trails can be poorly marked, weather changes rapidly, and local knowledge of rescue routes is vital. WildWays Global provides experienced, certified guides on all trek packages.
