Kashmir 7-Day Itinerary — Complete Day-by-Day Plan

📅 Updated 2025 · ⏱ 8 min read · ✍️ WildWays Global Team

Seven days is the ideal duration for a first Kashmir trip — enough time to experience Srinagar's cultural depth, Gulmarg's mountain drama, Pahalgam's river valleys, and Sonamarg's glacial splendour without rushing. Here is WildWays Global's recommended 7-day Kashmir itinerary, used by thousands of our guests.

📋 Quick Summary: Day 1–2: Srinagar. Day 3: Gulmarg. Day 4: Srinagar to Pahalgam. Day 5: Pahalgam to Sonamarg. Day 6: Free/Shopping. Day 7: Departure.

Day 1 — Arrival in Srinagar

Arrive at Sheikh ul-Alam International Airport, Srinagar. Our WildWays driver meets you at arrivals. Check into your hotel or Dal Lake houseboat. In the evening, take a sunset shikara ride on Dal Lake — watching the Himalayas reflected in the still water as the light turns golden. Welcome dinner at a traditional Kashmiri restaurant.

Day 2 — Srinagar City Sightseeing

Full day in Srinagar: Shankaracharya Temple for panoramic city views, Mughal Gardens (Nishat Bagh, Shalimar Bagh, Chashme Shahi), the historic Hazratbal Shrine, and Pari Mahal. Evening at Lal Chowk for Kashmiri handicraft shopping — pashmina shawls, papier-mâché, carpets, saffron.

Day 3 — Day Trip to Gulmarg

Early morning drive to Gulmarg (56 km, 1.5–2 hrs). Ride Asia's highest cable car — the Gulmarg Gondola — to Phase 1 (Kongdori, 3,100m) and Phase 2 (Apharwat Peak, 4,390m). In winter, enjoy skiing and snow activities. In summer, walk the Khilanmarg meadows carpeted with wildflowers. Return to Srinagar for dinner.

Day 4 — Drive to Pahalgam

After breakfast, drive to Pahalgam (96 km via NH1). En route, stop at the Saffron fields of Pampore and Awantipora ruins (8th-century temple complex). Arrive Pahalgam and explore Betaab Valley (named after the Bollywood film) and Aru Valley. Overnight at a Pahalgam resort with Lidder River views.

Day 5 — Pahalgam & Sonamarg

Morning at Pahalgam — visit Chandanwari (starting point of the Amarnath Yatra). Then drive to Sonamarg (87 km from Srinagar). The "Meadow of Gold" sits at 2,740m with dramatic Himalayan scenery. Visit Thajiwas Glacier by pony (highly recommended) or on foot. Return to Srinagar for the night.

Day 6 — Free Day / Activities

A relaxed day in Srinagar. Options: second shikara ride, Kashmiri Wazwan cooking class, carpet weaving demonstration, visit to a papier-mâché workshop, or simply explore the old city's lanes. Evening: farewell Wazwan dinner with all 36 dishes of the royal Kashmiri feast.

Day 7 — Departure

After breakfast, our driver transfers you to Srinagar Airport. Take home: memories of Kashmir's eternal beauty, a packet of Kashmiri saffron, and hopefully a promise to return.

7 days is ideal for covering the four main destinations — Srinagar, Gulmarg, Pahalgam, and Sonamarg. If you want to include the Kashmir Great Lakes Trek, plan for 12–14 days total.
