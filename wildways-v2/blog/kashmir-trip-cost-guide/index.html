Kashmir Trip Cost 2025 — Honest Budget Breakdown

📅 Updated 2025 · ⏱ 8 min read · ✍️ WildWays Global Team

"How much will a Kashmir trip actually cost?" This is the second most-asked question we receive (after "Is Kashmir safe?"). The honest answer: Kashmir can cost anything from ₹8,000 to ₹1,00,000 per person depending on your choices. Here is a complete transparent breakdown.

Quick Summary: Budget traveller (4N/5D): ₹8,000–₹12,000 all-in. Mid-range (5N/6D): ₹18,000–₹28,000. Luxury (7N/8D): ₹45,000–₹80,000. Prices exclude airfare.

Flights to Srinagar

Srinagar Airport (SXR) is well-connected from Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, Kolkata, and Hyderabad.

  • Delhi → Srinagar (cheapest): ₹2,500–₹5,000 one way (book 6–8 weeks ahead)
  • Mumbai → Srinagar: ₹3,500–₹7,000 one way
  • Bangalore → Srinagar: ₹4,000–₹9,000 one way
  • Book on IndiGo, Air India, SpiceJet, or Vistara for best rates

Food Costs in Kashmir

  • Budget: ₹300–₹600/day (dhabas and local restaurants)
  • Mid-range: ₹800–₹1,500/day (hotel meals and tourist restaurants)
  • Luxury: ₹2,000–₹4,000/day (resort dining, Wazwan feasts)
  • Most WildWays packages include breakfast and dinner — saving ₹600–₹1,200/day
