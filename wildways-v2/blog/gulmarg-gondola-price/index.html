Gulmarg Gondola Price 2025 — Full Ticket Guide

📅 Updated 2025 · ⏱ 5 min read · ✍️ WildWays Global Team

The Gulmarg Gondola — officially the Gulmarg Ropeway — is the most iconic attraction in Kashmir. As one of the world's highest cable cars, it lifts visitors from the Gulmarg meadows (2,650m) up to Apharwat Peak (4,390m) in two spectacular phases.

2025 Gondola Prices: Phase 1 — ₹900/person. Phase 2 — ₹1,050/person. Both Phases — ₹1,850/person. Prices are set by J&K Cable Car Corporation and subject to annual revision.

Phase 1 — Gulmarg to Kongdori (₹900)

Phase 1 takes you from Gulmarg base station (2,650m) to Kongdori station (3,100m) — a 450-metre vertical rise in approximately 10 minutes. The ride offers sweeping views of the Gulmarg meadows, pine forests, and the surrounding Himalayan peaks.

  • Distance: 2.5 km
  • Duration: ~10 minutes
  • Gondola capacity: 8 persons per cabin
  • Price 2025: ₹900 per person
  • Operates: Year-round (weather permitting)

Phase 2 — Kongdori to Apharwat Peak (₹1,050)

Phase 2 is the spectacular upper section, rising from Kongdori (3,100m) to Apharwat Peak (4,390m) — a further 1,290-metre vertical ascent. At the top, the views of the entire Kashmir range — including Nanga Parbat in Pakistan on clear days — are extraordinary.

  • Distance: 2.7 km
  • Duration: ~13 minutes
  • Price 2025: ₹1,050 per person
  • Operates: December–April (winter ski season) primarily; May–November weather dependent

How to Book Gulmarg Gondola Tickets in 2025

There are two ways to book gondola tickets:

  1. On-site at Gulmarg: Buy tickets at the gondola ticket counter. Queue times: 30 min (off-peak), 2–4 hours (peak season like December–January and May–June weekends).
  2. Through WildWays Global: We pre-book gondola tickets as part of all Gulmarg tour packages. Guaranteed slots, no queuing. This is the recommended option for December–March and May–June visits.

Phase 2 depends on weather and snow conditions. It is most reliably open December–April. In summer (May–September), Phase 2 may close on windy or foggy days. Phase 1 is more reliably open year-round.

Yes — the Gulmarg Gondola is operated by J&K Cable Car Corporation and is regularly maintained. It is one of the most-visited cable cars in Asia with an excellent safety record.
