Our Story

Who We Are

WildWays Global — also known as Adventures Kashmir on TripAdvisor — was founded in 2018 with a single belief: that Kashmir's breathtaking beauty deserves to be shared with the world by people who truly call it home.

We are a Gulmarg-based travel agency — not a Delhi office with subcontracted Kashmir guides. Our founders, guides, and support team all live in Tangmarg and Gulmarg year-round. We experience every Kashmir season — the record snowfall winters, the wildflower summers, the golden autumns — and our tours reflect that intimacy with the land.

Since 2018, we have conducted 287+ tours and treks for guests from across India, UK, USA, Germany, Australia, Japan, and beyond. Our 4.9★ TripAdvisor rating reflects not just beautiful destinations but the trust our guests place in us — and our commitment to earning that trust on every single trip.

We are a Government of J&K registered tour operator and members of the J&K Tourism network.
