Kashmir Itinerary 2025

Planning a Kashmir trip? These are our tried-and-tested itineraries used by thousands of travellers. Pick the duration that suits you and let WildWays Global handle everything else.

Kashmir 5-Day Itinerary

  1. Arrival Srinagar — Dal Lake Shikara
    Arrive Srinagar airport. Transfer to hotel/houseboat. Evening shikara ride on Dal Lake. Welcome dinner.
  2. Srinagar Sightseeing — Mughal Gardens
    Shankaracharya Temple, Nishat Bagh, Shalimar Bagh, Chashme Shahi. Evening at Lal Chowk market.
  3. Srinagar → Gulmarg → Srinagar
    Day trip to Gulmarg (56 km). Gondola Phase 1 & Phase 2. Khilanmarg meadow walk. Return to Srinagar.
  4. Srinagar → Pahalgam
    Drive to Pahalgam (96 km). Betaab Valley, Aru Valley, Chandanwari. Overnight Pahalgam.
  5. Pahalgam → Srinagar → Departure
    Morning in Pahalgam. Drive back to Srinagar for afternoon flight. Shopping for Kashmiri handicrafts.

Kashmir 7-Day Itinerary

  1. Arrival Srinagar — Dal Lake
    Arrive, check in houseboat, evening shikara ride, welcome dinner.
  2. Srinagar City Tour
    Mughal Gardens, Shankaracharya Temple, Hazratbal Shrine, old city bazaars.
  3. Day Trip to Gulmarg
    Gondola, skiing/snow activities, Khilanmarg trek. Return to Srinagar.
  4. Srinagar → Pahalgam
    Drive via Pampore saffron fields and Awantipora ruins. Betaab Valley, Aru Valley.
  5. Pahalgam to Sonamarg
    Drive to Sonamarg (87 km from Srinagar). Thajiwas Glacier pony ride. Back to Srinagar.
  6. Free Day / Shopping in Srinagar
    Kashmir handicrafts, carpet weaving demo, Wazwan cooking class option. Farewell dinner.
  7. Departure
    Transfer to Srinagar airport. Fly onward with memories of Jannat.
