Top Things to Do in Pahalgam

🏞️
Betaab Valley

Named after the 1983 Bollywood film. 15 km from Pahalgam, stunning river valley.

🌿
Aru Valley

12 km from Pahalgam. Lush meadows, base for Kolahoi Glacier & Tarsar Marsar trek.

🚣
River Rafting

Lidder River rafting from Pahalgam — Grade II–III rapids, suitable for beginners.

❄️
Chandanwari Snow

16 km from Pahalgam. Snow point accessible even in summer. Start of Amarnath Yatra.

Pahalgam FAQs

Pahalgam is approximately 96 km from Srinagar — a 2.5 to 3 hour drive through the beautiful Kashmir valley. WildWays provides AC cab service on this route from ₹2,800 one-way.

Pahalgam is beautiful year-round. April–June: Lush green valleys, ideal weather. July–September: Trekking season, Amarnath Yatra period (July). October–November: Golden autumn, fewer crowds. December–March: Cold but scenic snow.
